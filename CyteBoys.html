What is this page?


This page should ideally contain all traps from various anime or games. Contains their full name, where appears and their image. It's basically a collection. Doesn't currently contain all traps, but will increase in number over time.

Who is trap?


Trap is a slang term used in online anime communities to describe a biologically male character with an attractive andrognynous or feminine appearance, and is often performed by voice actresses.

The word 'trap' carries unfortunate implications, as it implies that their femininity is used to "trap" (i.e. deceive) men into becoming an object of their desires, only to find out that the "girl" they want is a man all along, which makes them "gay".
